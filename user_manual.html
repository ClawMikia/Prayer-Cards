Welcome to Prayer Cards

Prayer Cards is a beautifully designed mobile application created to help you maintain a consistent and meaningful prayer life. With 31 unique prayer cards, it guides you through a month of spiritual reflection and daily communication with God.

The app's clean, minimalist aesthetic ensures that your focus remains on the spiritual content, providing a distraction-free environment for your daily devotions.

The Main Screen

When you open the app, you are greeted with the main prayer list. Here you can see all 31 prayer cards organized for easy access.

  • Header: Displays the inspiring title "31 Prayer Cards for your Everyday Life".
  • Prayer Cards: Each card represents a day of the month. Tap any card to view its content.
  • Remaining Count: At the bottom, you can track how many prayers you have left for the month.

Daily Prayer Content

Tapping on a prayer card opens the detailed prayer view. This screen is designed to be calm and readable, allowing the words to resonate.

  • Day Indicator: Clearly shows which day's prayer you are reading.

  • Source Info: Shows the origin or inspiration for the prayer content.

Progress Tracking

Prayer Cards helps you stay committed to your spiritual journey. Once you finish reading and reflecting on a daily prayer, tap the "Done Praying" button.

The app will automatically update your progress on the main screen, helping you visualize your consistency throughout the month. This simple interaction reinforces your daily habit and encourages you to complete all 31 cards.

About & Inspiration

The "About" page provides background information on the purpose of these prayer cards and the inspiration behind them.

It includes meaningful quotes and guidance on how to make the most of your prayer time, ensuring that the app is more than just a list of texts, but a companion for your faith journey.
